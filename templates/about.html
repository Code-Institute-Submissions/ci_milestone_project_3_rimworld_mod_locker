About

Rimworld Mod Locker aims to bring all the most popular mods for Rimworld into one catelogue that can be browsed by mod category, a browsing option severly lacking from Steam. Users can also create an account and be able to add mods to the different categories.

RimWorld is an indie top-down sci-fi colony sim driven by an intelligent AI storyteller. Inspired by Dwarf Fortress, Firefly, and Dune. It was created by Montreal-based developer Ludeon Studios. You can buy the game here.

This is a fan made site and not affliated with Ludeon Studios. All copyrights are their own.
